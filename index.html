Research & Team (研究方向与团队)

I am a proud member of the Radar Research Institute at BIT, guided by professor XiaopengYang. My research topic includes mathematical and modeling theory of signal processing, through-the-wall radar human activity recognition. Our team is dedicated to advancing the frontiers of urban radar technologies and applications.

我是北京理工大学雷达技术研究院的博士生，师从杨小鹏教授。我的研究方向为信号处理中的数学与建模理论，穿墙雷达人体行为识别技术。我们团队致力于拓展城市雷达技术与应用的研究边界。
